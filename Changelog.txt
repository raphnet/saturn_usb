	- Initial release under GPLv2. 
	- Supports 1 alternate mapping. Hold 'Start' at power-up.


	Connect Clock to PC5
	Connect Latch to PC4
	Connect Data to PC3
	Connect Pin2 (+5volts) to 5 volts
	Connect Ground to Ground...

	Do not connect Vbat.


Pin  Color   Name
-----------------------------------
 1   Yellow  Data
 2   Blue    +5 volts
 3   Brown   Reset (Latch)
 4   Orange  Clock
 5   Black   Ground
 6   Red     Vbat (battery power)
